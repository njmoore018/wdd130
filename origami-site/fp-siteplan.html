Site Name:

Into the Fold

Site Purpose

Do you ever find yourself with some small amount of free time and nothing to do with it? What do you normally do, look at your phone? That's not very beneficial for you. What if you could create something cool and unique in that time instead? You could stand out from all the people who sit and stare at their phones all the time, and all you need is a few pieces of paper. My plan is to create a website to share simple origami designs and tutorials that anyone can follow and recreate. You could amaze your friends simply by folding paper, and you would certainly feel better than if you sat around and did nothing. Even in the worst-case scenario where nobody is impressed by the work of art you've created; you can at least enjoy your own creation and sense of satisfaction from learning a new skill.

Site Map

Home Page

The home page will contain an introduction to the website, as well as links to the two sub pages. There will be a small menu to contain these links within the header of each page, and a footer to contain links to social media pages. The home page will also contain pictures of some popular origami designs and some words to excite visitors (maybe including a call to action on one of the images).

Tutorial Videos

The tutorials page will contain origami creations each with the name, a photo, and a tutorial video for how to make it yourself. This page can be updated frequently with new and more exciting creations as submissions come in and new folding methods are learned. These videos will be homemade by myself and likely uploaded to YouTube with a link to the video on included on the page.

User Submissions

The submissions page will contain submissions from visitors to the site. There will be a method for submitting photos at the bottom of this page (likely an email address). I'd also like to include a method for visitors to "like" or "upvote" certain submissions, but that may be outside my capabilities at the moment.
