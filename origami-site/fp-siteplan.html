Site Name:

Into the Fold

Site Purpose

Do you ever find yourself with some small amount of free time and nothing to do with it? What do you normally do, look at your phone? That's not very beneficial for you. What if you could create something cool and unique in that time instead? You could stand out from all the people who sit and stare at their phones all the time, and all you need is a few pieces of paper. My plan is to create a website to share simple origami designs and tutorials that anyone can follow and recreate. You could amaze your friends simply by folding paper, and you would certainly feel better than if you sat around and did nothing. Even in the worst-case scenario where nobody is impressed by the work of art you've created; you can at least enjoy your own creation and sense of satisfaction from learning a new skill.
