Site Name

Go with the Flow River Rafting

Site Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the site will be to encourage people searching for a white water rafting adventure to book a trip with our company.

Target Audience

Scenarios

  • Families looking for a rafting adventure.
  • Young adult groups planning trips together.
  • Organizations like churches planning fun activities for their members.
  • Solo riders looking for an adventure (This can be an opportunity for them to meet new friends as well)
